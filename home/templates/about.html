TreeBooks is a small charity and out mission is to raise money to help reforest our planet.

How we do it?

We collect second hand books that people donate and then we rent them for a small fee in order to raise money. The money collected is then used to help in reforestation projects.

How can you collaborate?

By reading second hand books you are reducing the number of trees that are cut to create new books.

By paying us to rent a book instead of buying a brand new book you are contributing to reforest the planet.

You can also donate your second hand books to help us growing the collection and offer more choice to our members. (See our collection points)

Spread the word and help us get more members. More member reading our books means less trees being cut.
